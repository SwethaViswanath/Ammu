About Us

Early in 1964,the enlightened public of Kanjangad and Nileshwar resolved to honour the memory bof Javaharlal Nehru ,the architect of modern India by undertaking educational and cultural activities in this educationally backward area.In pursuit of this idea, a dedicated and learner team setup in 1966,an educational society known as The Nehru Memorial Education Society under the distinguished presidentship of the late Shri c.k.Nair following which the society was duly registered.The establishment of Nehru Arts nd science College,Kanhangad in this backward coastaltract was the major task the society had set before themselves and had subsequently accomplished it.
